About the Vector Smart Chain Hackathon

Welcome to the Vector Smart Chain (VSC) Hackathon—an ongoing opportunity for developers, innovators, and blockchain enthusiasts to build, compete, and showcase their skills on our cutting-edge testnet. Designed to encourage creativity and innovation, the VSC Hackathon is your gateway to building dApps that push the boundaries of Web3 technology.

The VSC Hackathon is structured to be dynamic and ever-evolving. Our testnet serves as the development ground for participants, offering a real-world environment to build and deploy decentralized applications (dApps) with ease. Each month, we introduce three new categories, allowing developers to explore and innovate across a range of fields such as DeFi, AI, NFTs, and more. This rotating structure ensures that there is always something fresh to explore, providing a continuous opportunity to test and showcase the most creative solutions.

Monthly Categories and Prizes

Every month, we will spotlight new challenges across different categories, encouraging participants to push their limits and experiment with the latest blockchain and AI technology trends. By adding three new categories each month, we aim to provide diverse opportunities for developers with different interests and expertise. Whether you’re building a smart contract, launching an NFT platform, or developing an AI-based financial tool, the VSC Hackathon offers the perfect platform to create, learn, and win.

Join the VSC Community

The VSC Hackathon is not just about competition; it’s about collaboration and growth. By participating, you’ll gain access to our developer community, mentorship programs, and a wide range of resources designed to support you every step of the way. Stay tuned for updates, new categories, and prize announcements each month—there’s always something new happening at the VSC Hackathon.
